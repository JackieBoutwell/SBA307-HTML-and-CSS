Services

Here are some general guidelines for making an appointment here at the salon:

  • Confirm Appointment: We will send a confirmation message (via text, email, or phone call) a day or two before your appointment. Make sure to confirm your appointment to ensure everything is set.
  • Arrive on Time: Respect the salon's schedule by arriving on time for your appointment. Arriving late may result in your appointment being shortened or rescheduled, especially if there are other clients scheduled after you.
  • Know What You Want: Have a clear idea of the services you need or want. Whether it's a haircut, color treatment, styling, or any other service, knowing in advance helps the salon staff.
